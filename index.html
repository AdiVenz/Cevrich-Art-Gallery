About

Cevrich Jan M. Octaviano AKA "Jaki", a 21 year old Filipino who was born in Milan, Italy in 2001. He studied in a public middle school called G.B Vico while spending most of his free time on learning digital art, a fan of drawing ever since his childhood days, he begins to start learning through his phone making sketches and character illustrations. One day his family decided to transfer to the Philippines in Lucban, Quezon in 2016 due to their budget problem where he begins to start Junior High School at Casa Del Niño Jesus De Lucban and finishing off with Senior High graduating from the STEM course. Later on he proceeded in focusing on his passion of digital drawing and apply in college at Southern Luzon State University of Lucban with Industrial Design Technology which improved and emphasized his art style and focus on character designs and even opened commissions to the public.
